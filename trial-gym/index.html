Trial At Your Gym Intero 2019-09-19T15:14:30+00:00

If your gym has Concept2 rowing and/or ski ergs, and you are interested in providing these applications to your customers, please email contact@interovr.com. We will lease you iPads for each machine which have the applications installed. We will provide you with an iPad holder as well. Your gym clients simply need to make an account and login each time they return to the gym. A representative will visit your gym and ensure that everything is is working smoothly.

REQUIREMENTS TO USE OUR SYSTEM

Concept2 Machines

Rower, SkiErg, or BikeErg of Concept2.

iOS devices

Currently we are only compatible with IOS devices, soon with Android.
